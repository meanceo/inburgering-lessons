## Content Rule

This lesson explains before it tests. Each grammar section must include:

1. a short English explanation,
2. clear Dutch examples with English meaning where useful,
3. short A2 practice tasks,
4. answers immediately under the task,
5. a short explanation of why the answer is correct.

For word-order tasks, the slash-separated words must be genuinely shuffled and must not already appear in the correct answer order.
